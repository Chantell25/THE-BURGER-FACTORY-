Our Story

From Small Kitchen to TikTok Fame

TheBurgerFactory began in 2023 as a small family-owned burger joint in Polokwane. What started as a passion project quickly grew into a local sensation thanks to our commitment to quality ingredients and innovative burger creations.

Our big break came when a customer posted a video of our massive "Burger Tower Challenge" on TikTok. The video went viral, garnering over 2 million views in just one week.
